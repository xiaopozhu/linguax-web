# Privacy Policy

**Your data belongs to you. LinguaX doesn't collect, process, or forward any personally identifiable data on to a third party.**

* LinguaX doesn't use any trackers or send any personally identifiable data to a third party.
* LinguaX uses a third-party service to facilitate purchases made in the app. Purchase information is sent anonymously and is not linked to you, and cannot be used to identify you.
