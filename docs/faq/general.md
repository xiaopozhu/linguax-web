## What is LinguaX?

LinguaX is a macOS utility for automatic input-source switching by app and website, with Mouse+ enhancement.

## Who is it for?

Developers, designers, and multilingual users who switch contexts frequently.

## Does LinguaX only work in browsers?

No. It supports app-level rules across macOS apps. Browser domain rules are an additional layer.

## Why is a website rule not triggering?

Most cases are caused by one of these:

- incorrect domain text
- overlapping rules
- missing macOS permissions

See [Browser Domain Rules](../guides/browser-domain-rules.md) and [Common Issues](../troubleshooting/common-issues.md).

## Does LinguaX require cloud processing for core behavior?

No. Core switching logic runs locally on your Mac.
